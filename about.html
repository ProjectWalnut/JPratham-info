About

Hello! I’m Prathamesh! I’m the kind of person who loves diving into tough problems and figuring out how to solve them. Whether it's building solid backend systems that can handle scale or experimenting with AI, I’m always asking myself one question: How can tech make life better?

Currently pursuing my Master’s in Artificial Intelligence at Yeshiva University in the heart of Midtown Manhattan, I’m diving into AI techniques like Vision Transformers, LLMs, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation. I’m particularly passionate about the intersection of distributed systems and AI, and I love sharing my discoveries through my YouTube channel, Chief Tech O, where I make tech, AI, and system design accessible and fun.

Beyond tech, I have a deep love for Quantum Physics as well as Classical Physics. I find it fascinating how the laws of nature shape everything around us—much like how tech can change the world. After physics, you’ll usually find me gaming (Valorant & Call of Duty are my go-to) as a way to unwind and stay grounded.

I believe curiosity is the ultimate superpower, and there’s always something new to discover, build, or share. So, if you’re passionate about tech, gaming, or the wonders of the universe, let’s connect—because the best breakthroughs often start with a spark of curiosity.

I also really enjoy solving problems on LeetCode. It’s a great way for me to challenge myself and keep sharpening my skills. Feel free to check out my profile below!
